About the course

An 8‑week live cohort, Generative AI for Work & Research focuses on no‑code tools, practical automations, and communication for research impact. Led by instructors and mentors from the GRAPH Network, University of Geneva.

Bootcamp features
  • Weekly live workshops & help sessions
  • Personalized feedback & capstone coaching
  • Private community + lifetime tutorial access
  • Shareable certificate
Grading rubric
  • Online quizzes — 15%
  • Assignments — 35%
  • Attendance & participation — 10%
  • Final project — 40% (pass ≥ 80%)
Attendance
  • Attend ≥ 5 workshops for full credit
  • Global, supportive learning ecosystem

Modules at a glance

Module 1 — Websites, Documents & Presentations: Generate Markdown/LaTeX/HTML, build simple research sites, and craft clear slides.
Module 2 — AI‑Automated Data Analysis: Use ChatGPT Code Interpreter, Julius & TheBricks to process data and create automated reports.
Module 3 — GenAI for Research & Text Analysis: Literature reviews with Elicit/LiteRev, text classification & summarization workflows.
Module 4 — Custom GPTs & Chatbots: Chat architecture, context windows, RAG, and Botpress integrations.
Module 5 — AI for Task Automation: Email automations, agent‑based flows, and browser control agents.
Module 6 — Communication & Outreach: Figures/diagrams, video abstracts, and scheduling content for dissemination.
Module 7 — Capstone & Showcase: Progress checks, peer feedback, and public presentations.
